HackForGood

Evenings of Hacking to make the world a better place

What:

Imagine if for once we decided to write software and web applications that do not revolve only around technology, social networking or sharing pictures but rather focus on improving the life of those around us.

The idea behind the HackForGood evenings is to gather the best developers in certain cities, sit together, write applications that could be used for the greater good and that can help other human beings or the environment depending on the theme of the night.

The rules are very simple. Have a good time, have a few drinks, eat some food, write some code, enjoy.
